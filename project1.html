Rory's Wisdom

The Challenge

The client, a dog trainer and behaviorist, needed a brand identity that was both professional and deeply personal. The business name is an homage to his late dog, Rory, who was the inspiration for his career. The brief required a unique mark that visually merged a representation of Rory with the owner’s signature circular glasses.

The Solution

The goal was to translate this heartfelt story into a trustworthy brand. The final design creates a single, memorable icon that symbolizes “Rory’s wisdom seen through the expert eyes of the trainer”, instantly communicating the brand’s core values of empathy, legacy, and expertise.
